For the people who love Paws

PawFinder is a cat-focused registered charity and humane society with a no-kill mandate.
Since 2000, we've been rescuing and facilitating the adoption of stray and abandoned cats into new, loving permanent homes.

We work hard to ensure that each MEOW cat is matched with the best possible home based on his or her personality, history, habits and individual needs.
